Question: A train 100 meters long is running at a speed of 60 km/h. How much time will it take to cross a pole?

  1. 5 seconds
  2. 6 seconds
  3. 10 seconds

Correct Answer: 2. 6 seconds

Reason: The time taken to cross a pole is calculated using the formula: Time = Distance ÷ Speed. The speed is first converted to meters per second: 60 km/h ÷ 3.6 = 16.67 m/s, and then 100 m ÷ 16.67 m/s ≈ 6 seconds.
