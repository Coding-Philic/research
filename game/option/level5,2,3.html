Quiz Question

Question: Which of the following is an example of a liability?

  1. Money in a savings account
  2. A loan taken from a bank
  3. A car owned outright

Correct Answer: 2. A loan taken from a bank

Reason: A liability is a financial obligation, such as a loan, that you are required to repay. In contrast, money in a savings account and a car owned outright are assets, as they represent items of value you own.
