Quiz Question

Question: Which process is used to produce large quantities of DNA in the lab?

  1. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction)
  2. Photosynthesis
  3. Respiration

Correct Answer: 1. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction)

Reason: PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) is a laboratory technique used to amplify small segments of DNA, producing large quantities. Photosynthesis and respiration are biological processes, but they do not serve to replicate DNA in the lab.
