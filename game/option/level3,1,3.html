Quiz Question

Question: What is the main function of ribosomes in a cell?

  1. DNA replication
  2. Protein synthesis
  3. Energy production

Correct Answer: 2. Protein synthesis

Reason: Ribosomes are responsible for protein synthesis in the cell. They read messenger RNA (mRNA) and translate it into amino acid sequences to form proteins. DNA replication and energy production are carried out by other cellular structures like the nucleus and mitochondria, respectively.
