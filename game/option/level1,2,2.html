Question: What is the purpose of a savings account?

  1. To store money and earn interest
  2. To take loans
  3. To buy stocks

Correct Answer: 1. To store money and earn interest

Reason: The primary purpose of a savings account is to store money safely and earn interest over time. It is a low-risk option for individuals to keep their money while earning a small return. Taking loans or buying stocks is not the primary function of a savings account.
