Question: If a car travels 90 km in 2 hours, what is its average speed?

  1. 45 km/h
  2. 90 km/h
  3. 180 km/h

Correct Answer: 1. 45 km/h

Reason: The average speed is calculated using the formula: Average Speed = Total Distance รท Total Time. In this case, 90 km รท 2 hours = 45 km/h.
