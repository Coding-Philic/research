Question: What is the purpose of a vaccine?

  1. To provide nutrients to the body
  2. To stimulate the immune system
  3. To increase metabolism

Correct Answer: 2. To stimulate the immune system

Reason: The main purpose of a vaccine is to stimulate the immune system to recognize and fight pathogens such as viruses or bacteria. This helps the body develop immunity against the disease. Vaccines do not provide nutrients or increase metabolism.
