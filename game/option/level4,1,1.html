Quiz Question

Question: Which of the following is used in making insulin through biotechnology?

  1. Bacteria
  2. Fungi
  3. Virus

Correct Answer: 1. Bacteria

Reason: Bacteria, specifically genetically modified E. coli, are commonly used in biotechnology to produce insulin. The gene for human insulin is inserted into the bacterial DNA, which then produces insulin. Fungi and viruses can also be used in biotechnology, but bacteria are most commonly employed for insulin production.
